About Us

Our Company's Purpose

Our mission is to inform our clients about the various dance workshops that is being held in thier neighborhood. Our Company's goal is to inform our clients about the prices, locations, and all necessary details related to the program

Our Target audience

Our target audience are those who are interested in dance. Mostly people who engage in dance in our company are youngsters aged from 10-34 years old. Our audience belief that dancing is a good and a fun way to exercise. So, people who are willing to exercise usually take part in such workshops.

Our Instructors

We have very well talented and motivated instructors who are willing to coperate with you. They are friendly and approachable.
